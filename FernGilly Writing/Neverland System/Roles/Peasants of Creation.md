[[Peasant|Peasants]] of the [[Suits|Suit]] of [[Creation]] are [[Mortals]] strongly influenced by the [[Narrative Awareness System|Goddess of Suffering, N'as]]. Beloved children of the Goddess of Suffering and cared for under her embrace, each Peasant has manifested one of her [[Blessings]]; these Blessings reflect *how* that Peasant reflects the nature and beauty of N'as and her [[Sephirot]].
* [[Consorts of Creation]] - see *Consort of Creation*.
* Second Creative - [[Creation|Creatives]] of [[Diresk]] may always know a way forward, imbued with a supernatural confidence in their sense of direction.
* Third Creative - Creatives of [[Yedrin]] may find inspiration in nature, able to focus in on natural phenomena to make crystal clear observations.
* Fourth Creative - Creatives of [[Seprix]] are able to find opportunities anywhere they look, even in the midst of danger and disaster.
* Fifth Creative - Creatives of [[Tracta]] can leverage their talents to open doors closed to others, using their art as a means to an end.
* Sixth Creative - Creatives of [[Remora]] are able to vividly impress their own stories upon readers, and their words linger in the ears of their listeners.
* Seventh Creative - Creatives of [[Netrim]] are powerful wheelers and dealers, quick to make friends and quick to earn confidence.
* Eighth Creative - Creatives of [[Bassur]] are focused on social change, able to identify and take in the viewpoints of other communities.
* Ninth Creative - Creatives of [[Mysten]] are able to make moving works of art that connect with others on a fundamental level.
* Tenth Creative - Creatives of [[Dracoa]] are opportunities in and of themselves, providing work and resources to other [[Actors]].
