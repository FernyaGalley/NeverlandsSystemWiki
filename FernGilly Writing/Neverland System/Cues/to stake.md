*Tenses (v): staked (past), stake (present), staking (progressive)*
*Laconic: To raise the stakes of Conflict.* 
## Lore

To **stake** an [[Game Mechanics|Element]] is to offer it and raise the stakes of [[Conflicts]]. This is a [[Cues|Cue]] which requires [[Discomfort|consent]] from the other [[Players]] at the [[Tables|Table]], but it is an effective way [[to subvert]] an [[Dances|Apocalypse]]; by staking, an [[Actors|Actor]] effectively offers an all-or-nothing gamble to the other [[Cults|Cult]].

[[to pin|Pinning]] [[Actors|Actors]] while the stakes are raised will result in catastrophic losses for their Cult, so Actors are often filled with determination they might not usually have. This is almost certainly the climax of the [[Acts|Act]]; remember that any staked Elements will be divided among all remaining Cults in Conflict [[Rule of Fairness|equitably]].
## Mechanics of Staking

**Mechanically,** [[to target|targeted]] [[Game Mechanics|Elements]] are [[to introduce|introduced]] with [[to stake|(Staked)]] and every [[to enjoin|(Enjoined)]] [[Cults|Cult]] in [[Conflicts|Conflict]] is required to stake [[Assets]] of similar collective value to the [[to target|(Targeted)]] Elements. The [[Directors]] [[to weigh|weigh]] each offering, and if the stakes seem [[Appropriateness and Relevance|reasonable]] and the [[Tables|Table]] [[Discomfort|consents]], the staked Assets are also introduced with **(Staked)**.
## Feature - (Staked)
*see also [[Assets]] and [[Dances]]*

When **(Staked)** is [[to introduce|introduced]] to a [[Statements|Statement]] or [[Props|Prop]], then those [[Game Mechanics|Elements]] are now up for grabs in the current [[Conflicts|Conflict]]. If any [[Cults|Cult]] no longer has [[Actors]] in the Conflict, they lose all claims to everything which is (Raised); the remaining Cults will secure their Assets [[Rule of Fairness|equitably]].
## Mechanics of (Staked)
 
 **Mechanically,** [[to stake|(Staked)]] [[Assets]] in [[Canons|Canon]] [[to snuff|snuff]] any attempts [[to pin]] [[Cultists]] that are part of the [[Cults|Cult]] which currently [[Rule of Possessions|possesses]] said Assets. When [[Conflicts|Conflict]] [[to end|ends]], all [[to stake|(Staked)]] Assets and [[Game Mechanics|Elements]] are distributed [[Rule of Fairness|equitably]] among the Cults left standing; the [[Directors]] [[to arbitrate|arbitrate]] the [[Results]].
