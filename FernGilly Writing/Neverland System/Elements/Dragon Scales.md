Dragon Scales are any [[Stages]] or [[Positions]] that [[to defend|defend]] or [[to sever|sever]] a Dragon-Temple from other Dragon-Temples in that [[Constellations|Constellation]]. Scales can be natural boundaries, such as cliff-sides, rivers and mountain-sides. They can also be artificial boundaries; a stone wall, a wooden bridge or even another subordinate Dragon-Temple.

Scales often act as [[Risk and Duress|risky]] [[Crossovers]] requiring [[Actors]] who wish to pass to complete some rite or ritual. Some Dragon-Temples have permeable boundaries that any [[Mortals]] can [[to cross|cross]], like an open field with a simple sign at the city limits. Other Dragon-Temples choose [[to relocate]] the flow of traffic into predictable passages; that way they can charge tolls, verify identities and deter invasions.

Dragon-Temples use their Scales to keep [[Dragon Breath|Breath]] from fleeing their sphere of influence; a competently managed Dragon-Temple may have a unique ecosystem, and their boundaries will be defined by the edges at which their Breath ends and another Dragon-Temple's Breath begins.

Scales may only be breached or subverted with an [[Dances|Apocalypse]], allowing the [[Tables|Table]] time [[to respond]] to any attempt to destroy or reshape a Scale. This [[Dances|Apocalypse]] must be initiated by a [[Priestesses|Priestess]], either of this Dragon-Temple or an another [[(Adjacent)]] Dragon-Temple or [[Constellations|Constellation]].
