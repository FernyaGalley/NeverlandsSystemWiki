*Tenses (v): enjoined (past), enjoin (present), enjoining (infinitive)*
*Laconic: Join a Conflict.*

To **enjoin** a [[Conflicts|Conflict]] is to make a [[Statements|Statement]] to the [[Racks|Rack]] during an **[[Conflicts|Open Conflict State]]**. Sarcastic and witty remarks do not automatically put an [[Actors|Actor]] in Conflict with other Actors, but [[Directors]] may request an Actor join Conflict if it becomes clear they are attempting to strategize or help other Players at the [[Tables|Table]] during a Conflict they have not enjoined yet.

When an Actor enjoins a Conflict, they must also [[to claim|claim]] a [[Positions|Position]]. The Position they claim should be placed at the top of the Rack as an [[Alternatives|Alternative]] to a [[Foundations|Foundation]] in [[Canons|Canon]], and the Directors should [[to canonize|canonize]] this Alternative in a Closed State before returning to an Open State. **In simpler terms: Actors need to pick where they stand on the [[Stages|Stage]] after enjoining.**

## Feature - (Enjoined)
When [[to inflict|inflicted]] as a [[Conditions|Condition]], Enjoined forces an Actor to enjoin into Conflict, even if they didn't want to do so in the first place. Players must still consent to joining the Conflict. When an Enjoined Actor enjoins combat, they may take the Foundation in Canon as their Position **or** present an Alternative to the Rack. Enjoined Actors can still be [[to relocate|relocated]] from their relatively safe position, so they might need to defend the Foundation against Strikes and Statements.
