*Tenses (v): responded (past), respond (present), responding (progressive)*
*Laconic: To read a Statement.*

To **respond** to a [[Statements|Statement]] is to directly acknowledge a Statement and to follow up the acknowledged Statement with another [[Statements|Statement]], a [[Cues|Cue]] or a [[Strikes|Strike]]. [[Players]] are encouraged to respond to most or all [[Appropriateness and Relevance|relevant]] Statements.

Responding is a *staple* [[Game Mechanics|mechanic]] of the [[Neverlands System]] that the entire [[Tables|Table]] should be comfortable with; ignoring Statements made by other Players is impolite, if not downright *detrimental* to the success of the [[Narratives|Narrative]]. Reading the Statements others make is a basic courtesy to them!
## Feature - (Responded)
*see also*

When a [[Players|Player]] responds to a [[Statements|Statement]], the [[Directors|Director]] has been [[to imply|implied]] to have [[to introduce|introduced]] the (Responded) [[Features|Feature]] to that Statement. It is not a [[Appropriateness and Relevance|relevant]] Feature in normal play, but can be useful [[to denote]] when the Table is unsure if they have already addressed a Statement directly and there are many Statements to parse through on the [[Racks|Rack]] and in [[Canons|Canon]].
