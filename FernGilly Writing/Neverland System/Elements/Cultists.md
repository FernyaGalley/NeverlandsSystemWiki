*Laconic: Members of a Cult.*

A **Cultist** is an [[Actors|Actor]] who is allied with a [[Cults|Cult]]. Cultists don't need to agree with every decision a Cult makes; they enjoy the benefits of a Cult by merely agreeing to supply the Cult with some benefit themselves. This benefit can be a resource the Cult needs, a service provided to other Cultists at a steep discount, or labor conducted on behalf of the Cult; benefits provided by a Cultist should be preferential.

Cultists may borrow [[Assets]] from a Cult, provided that they are acting in accordance with any [[Hills]] the Cult has played to the [[Outlines]] this [[Acts|Act]]. If a Cultist fails to defend a Cult against an Apocalypse
