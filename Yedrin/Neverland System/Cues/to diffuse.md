*Tenses (v): (past), (present), (progressive)*
*Laconic: To snuff one's own Results to help another Actor.*
## Lore

To **diffuse** a [[Results|Result]] is to give up on a selfish victory for the benefit of others. Diffusing could be as simple as doubling back to save a rival's life, essentially giving up on the opportunity to win in exchange for something far more valuable. Diffusing can also be used to represent teamwork, essentially 'spending' one's own effort to make another [[Actors|Actor]] look like a hero.

Since diffusing requires sacrifice, [[Casters]] must always name their sacrifice
## Mechanics of Diffusing

**Mechanically,** diffusing [[to snuff|snuffs]] a [[Bright]] or [[Dim]] [[Results|Result]] to [[to burn|burn]] another Result. Diffusing is likely [[to pin]] [[Casters]], so Casters are [[to discourage|discouraged]] from diffusing their own [[Statements]].

Diffusing may be [[to cast|cast]] during [[Arbitration]] without additional cost, but sacrificing a [[to canonize|(Canonized)]] Statement to cast this [[Cues|Cue]] will return a [[Dull]] Result. Dull Results will almost always leave the Caster [[to pin|(Pinned)]], so diffuse wisely.
