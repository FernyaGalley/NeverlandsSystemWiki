*Laconic: The physics and laws that society have agreed to enforce.*

An **Accord** is a [[Lines|Line]] concerning the *physics* of a [[Dragon-Temples|Dragon-Temple]] or [[Constellations|Constellation]]. Dragon-Temples have more than just physics of motion; they have laws and they have cultural norms and expectations. Some Dragon-Temples even have their own unique forms of [[Rule of Magical Thinking|magical thinking]]; a [[Cults|Cult]] may [[to believe|believe]] that magic operates in one way magic from other Cults do not. Accords regulate that magic in plain, easy to decipher terms for [[Players]].

[[Actors]] don't usually have access to Accords. [[to reveal|To reveal]] the contents of an Accord, Actors will likely need to go to the source-- a university, a library, the Dragon-Temple's [[Dragon Heart|Heart]], or speak to one of the higher-ranking [[Priestesses]] with knowledge of the Accords. The [[Tables|Table]], on the other hand, has full knowledge of the Accords when [[Appropriateness and Relevance|reasonable]]; they may ask the [[Directors]] if something is against or in line with the Accords, and the Directors must be [[Theory of Honesty|honest]] about the content of the Accords where it matters.

Dragon-Temples may only change their Accords during [[Intermissions]], and changing their Accords counts as an [[Dances|Apocalypse]]. This prevents the Table from being caught off guard by changes to the physics and laws of a region without having any input into how the story proceeds. We strongly recommend that Directors avoid [[to improvise|improvising]] this [[Game Mechanics|Rules]].
