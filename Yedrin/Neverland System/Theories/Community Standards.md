*Laconic: The few things that can get your free licenses taken away.*

Generally speaking, our [[Attributions and Licenses]] policy is very generous. All of the Neverlands System is available for personal and commercial use with no need for kickbacks, royalties or creative oversight. We want people to use this project and want to give them the tools to be empowered to create new and interesting things. We also recognize, however, that [[Fascists|Fascists]] enjoy finding wholesome and creative projects and subverting them, and that we need some sort of protection against political campaigns using our project to promote war, child rape and other [[Discomfort|uncomfortable]] topics.

So, we're spelling it out here; these are the *things you're not allowed to do with our System, even given the permissive licenses.* By using one of our licenses, you open yourself up to legal liability if you attempt to do one of the following with our system:

- The use of the Neverland System to advocate for warfare, state-sponsored violence, state-sponsored terrorism or genocide.
- The use of the Neverland System to fuel and produce works for the purpose of depriving creatives and artists of their livelihood, eg. AI, Pinkerton, and CIA-funded death squads.
- The use of the Neverland System to produce works designed to maximize harm against a vulnerable group that lacks protections, eg. the production of CSAM and animal abuse pornography.
- The use of the Neverland System to promote something which is outwardly Fascist or designed to stoke the flames Fascist sentiment in its viewers. This includes works which are designed for the purpose of promoting Fascism but not works which incidentally support Fascist characters or ideas, which would be hard to monitor and dispute and therefore not worth our time.
- Any work which threatens all of human life, whether it be through climate catastrophe or nuclear holocaust.

Honestly, if you try to do some of these and you're somehow protected from legal liability through arcane loopholes, some of us are going to find a way to kill you. That's not a threat, that's a promise. It will be brutal, it will probably be televised and at least one of us will happily martyr themselves if it means protecting human lives and her hard work from your Fascist ideals. So, fuck off, Fascists, and find some other foxhole to squander your worthless lives on.
