*Laconic: Hard to avoid.*

When a [[Statements|Statement]] is **inevitable** in the [[Neverlands System]], it will always become [[Facts]] by the end of the [[Acts|Act]] during which that Statement is [[to reveal|revealed]]. No Rule or Mechanic may alter the course of the events; **(Inevitable)** Statements *must* occur. With that said:

**(Inevitable) Statements may be interpreted in multiple ways.**

There are many 'prophecies' which seem to spell inevitable failure, but the listener lacked important context. An ominous promise that the 'sun will be devoured by the earth' may instead refer to the sun setting every night; the movements of windmills may be mistaken for deadly giants. A Statement which is (Inevitable) may be superseded by an Alternative interpretation of a Statement which says essentially the same thing, at the Director's discretion.
