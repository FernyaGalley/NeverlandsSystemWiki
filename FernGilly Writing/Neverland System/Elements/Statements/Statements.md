# Fact 
---
A Fact is a Statement which has been determined by the [[Directors]] to be true. Truth as a philosophical concept is not something which can be measured by any mortal rule book, but when the [[Directors]] declares a Statement is Fact, then all [[Players]] should act as if they knew that Statement to be true beyond a doubt. True Statements are unlikely to be validated as false Statements, only obfuscated with lies and alternatives to the truth of reality.

With that said, things we know to be true about our reality now may not be true forever. If the Director determines that a Statement being treated as Fact detracts from the enjoyment and entertainment of the other [[Players]] at the [[Tables|Table]] they may retroactively change a Statement to be Fiction instead. This is a useful feature of Statements that can avoid many awkward or uncomfortable conversations. No Statement made within the context of the [[Neverlands System]] is immutable, and Players should be willing to re-evaluate their prior assumptions to come to new conclusions about reality and see how those new assumptions impact the [[Narratives]].
