A **Dull Result** is a [[Results]] which indicates complete or utter failure. Dull Results come about in many ways; an [[Actors|Actor]] performing techniques outside their realm of expertise is likely to fail, especially if they are performing under duress. Failure can also be caused when a [[Directors|Director]] plays a [[to snuff]] on a [[Dim]] Result and the [[Players|Player]] does not have [[Candles]] in their [[Pockets]].

Failure does not always imply a bad result; there are times in which a failure is preferable or even desirable. Narratives in which the hero always wins and never faces conflict tend to leave their readers drained of energy. Failure can bring risks, and risks draw the Table deeper into the [[Narratives]]. Some losses now can also breed success later; never forget that victories are made of more than a single [[Conflicts|Conflict]].
### Note: Avoiding Dull Results
Making a [[Statements|Statement]] will always carry the risk that your words will be misconstrued or used against you. With that said, it is more fun to make a Statement than to sit back and passively accept the status quo. Fascism is not beaten by sitting back and allowing them to perform evil deeds; a community cannot be built if the *community* is unwilling to cooperate. Have some fun and gamble! You'll never regret your decisions if you leave an interesting story behind.
