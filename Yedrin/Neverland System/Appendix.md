All of these are open-source textbooks in English designed to get Players familiar with entry-level philosophy, physics, mathematics, social studies, writing, medicine, history and science. Since the Neverlands System relies so heavily on logical debate and [[Facts|fact]], knowing basic facts about our own world can help play proceed quickly and efficiently. We encourage Players to try and understand more about our world and seek out even more information; the more you know, the richer your understanding of the world and her [[Miracles|miracles]] will be.

Thank you for spending time with us, in this world. We hope you find joy and meaning in the fight against fascist ideology, and the skills you learn here can be applied in your everyday life.
