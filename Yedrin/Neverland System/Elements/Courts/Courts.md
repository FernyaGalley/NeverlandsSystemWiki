The **Courts** are the roles [[Actors]] fill in a [[Narratives|Narrative]]. All [[Actors]] belong to at least one Court. Courts give Actors additional [[Cues]] they may [[to cast|cast]].

[[Peasant|Peasants]] and [[Consort|Prins]] compose the [[Lower Courts]], with each member of the Lower Court able to cast a series of [[Miracles]] based on their relationship to the [[Sephirot]] of the [[Narrative Awareness System]]. This blessing is faith-based, with each Peasant demonstrating a unique connection to their [[Sephirot|Sephirah]].

The [[Noble Courts]] are made of the [[Consort|Prin]], the [[Nomad]], the [[Knight]], the [[Maiden]] and the [[Witch]]. Nobles were once Peasants before making a deal with a [[Neverlanders|Neverlander]] and subsequently learning how to cast magic. Each Noble of the Court has a unique connection to the twenty magical emotions of the [[Narrative Awareness System]], and are able to influence others by relying on those emotions. The full spectrum of Miracles is also available to the Noble Courts, as they are specifically ordained by the [[Sephirot|Sephirah]] with godlike abilities.

Neverlanders, as imaginary creatures that live outside of space and time, do not have a single Court; they may choose [[to assume]] the role of any of the Lower Courts at the start of an [[Acts|Act]] and maintain that role until the end of the Act. This willingness to play different roles gives them the full breadth of abilities granted by the [[Narrative Awareness System]] to her blessed chosen.
