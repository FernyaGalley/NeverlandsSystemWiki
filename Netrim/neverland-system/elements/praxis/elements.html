Elements

Disambiguation: Elements, Mechanics, Notes, Theories and Poems are all forms of Praxis. For the magical elements of Mages, look at Suits and Courts.

Laconic: The components of a Narrative.

Elements are the components of a Narrative; they are the metaphorical and meta-textual forms of Praxis.

Identifying Elements

In all English Translations of the Neverlands System, the word Element is always notarized with capitalization, as are all Elements. If any Players observe a capitalized word and it is not the name of an Actor or the start of a Statement, they should suspect that it is an Element. Other Translations may have other methods for observing and identifying Elements in their language.

Understanding Elements

to help players easily identify Elements on sight. In this particular version of the Neverlands System, the first time an Element is referenced in an article it will also have a link to the page describing what that Element is and how to interpret Statements with those Elements.

Elements are the building blocks of a Narrative; its Actors, the Stages they play upon, the Dynamics they share and the Scenes they play in. Each individual component of the Narrative is an Element. Elements are used to represent and reference other concepts in the Narrative for ease of participation.
