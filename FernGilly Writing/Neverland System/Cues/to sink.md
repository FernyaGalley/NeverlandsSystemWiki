*Tenses (v): sinked (past), sink (present), sinking (progressive)*
*Laconic: To threaten someone with a Prop or Cue.*

To **sink** a [[Statements|Statement]] is to play it to the [[Sinks|Sink]], a special [[Pockets|Pocket]] that each [[Actors|Actor]] [[to enjoin|enjoined]] to [[Conflicts|Conflict]] possesses. Sinking a Statement prevents other [[Players]] from [[to manipulate|manipulating]] the Statement in any way, shape or form and [[to introduce|introduces]] the (Sinked) [[Features|Feature]] to that Statement.
## Feature - (Sinked)
*see also*

When a [[Statements|Statement]] has been [[to sink|(Sinked)]], it is now immune to [[to manipulate|manipulation]] for as long as the Statement remains in the [[Sinks|Sink]]. When a (Sinked) Statement in the Sink [[Pockets|Pocket]] is [[to resolve|resolved]], it is both [[Risk and Duress|safe]] and [[to burn|burned]]; it might as well be [[to canonize|canonized]]!
