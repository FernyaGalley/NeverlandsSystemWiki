*Tenses (v): held (past), hold (present), holding (progressive)*
*Laconic: To take a Position.*

To **hold** a [[Positions|Position]] is for an [[Actors|Actor]] to take a definitive stance that their [[Opinions|Opinion]] is that a Position is a [[to validate|valid]] [[Facts|Fact]]. Actors believe in this Position so strongly that they are willing to defend all linked Positions from  as well.

[[To relocate]] and hold a new Position [[to imply|implies]] the Actor is willing to be [[to pin|pinned]] to that Position if they cannot properly defend it. Every Actor holds at least one Position at the start of a [[Conflicts|Conflict]], and if they hold no Position at any point during Conflict they are given a [[response window]] in which they can hold a new Position; if they cannot grasp a new Position, they are (Pinned).
## Feature - (Held)
*see also [[to estrange|(Estranged)]] (antonym)* and its corollary [[to relocate|(Relocated)]]

When a [[Positions|Position]] is (Held), then it is held by at *least* one [[Actors|Actor]] in the [[Scenes|Scene]]. So long as any Actor holds a Position, they are [[to imply|implied]] to have [[to introduce|introduced]] (Held).
