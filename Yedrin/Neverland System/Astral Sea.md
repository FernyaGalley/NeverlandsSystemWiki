The **Astral Sea**, otherwise known as *outer space*, is the vast [[Stages|Stage]] upon which the [[Constellations]] rest. 

## The Foundations

The **Astral Sea** is a cold, hard vacuum. There are no sounds and there is no heat, because phonons are oscillations of molecules in sequence; molecule density is so thin that oscillations would be nearly imperceptible. It is densely irradiated; photons travel properly through the vacuum of space, exciting any molecules they touch. Since space has an extremely low density, it also has a low pressure, and the pressure of space decreases the boiling points of many materials. In space, since the boiling point of blood is lowered and photons excite any material they touch, contact with light is deadly; the blood wouldn't evaporate right out of your frozen skin, not quickly, but measures must be taken to protect oneself in space.

[[Mortals]] wear space suits (or [[Costumes]] with good [[Hazards|Hazard]] defenses) to isolate themselves from space; inside the suit, there is a relatively 'normal' pressure, no exposure to light and survivable warmth. This pressure extends to their space shuttles ([[Carriages]]) and space stations ([[Sanctuaries]]), which are meticulously designed spaces to keep an astronauts blood from boiling. Breathable air, nourishment, warmth and pressure are provided to Mortals within these spaces, allowing them to survive even in these inhospitable conditions.

Planets ([[Constellations]]) are extremely far apart; far enough entire generations could [[to breed|breed]] and [[to perish|perish]] in the time it takes to reach another solar system. There are no known or proven ways to fold space like a pretzel at time of writing, though it would be convenient for space colonization.
## The Alternatives

The **Astral Sea** is a *literal* sea, and any competent captain can [[to ferry|ferry]] a crew in her [[Dragon-Temples|Dragon-Temple]] [[to relocate]] their cargo between [[Constellations]]. Dragon-Temples are the preferred [[Carriages]] of the Astral sea, because they can properly supply the crew with [[Dragon Breath|Breath]] on their long journeys. Dragon-Temples also provide a [[Cults|Cult]] for the crew to center themselves around; fights and violence would often break out without the cohesion a religion provides.

Each Constellation in the Astral Sea sits further and further away from the last, but the [[Constellations|Neverlands]] are much closer than that; by the time the subsequent generations of [[Cultists]] reach the Neverlands centuries later, they should be delusional enough to comprehend their many wonders. Competent [[Priestesses]] and [[Divines]] could use [[Rule of Magical Thinking|magical thinking]] to shorten the journey to months, or within the crew's lifespan at least; smarter [[Mages]] and [[Mortals]] would prefer to travel to local [[Constellations]] instead, isles only a few months or years from [[Earth]]. There are, after all, millions of [[Dragon-Temples]] to explore in our own [[Solar System]]!
