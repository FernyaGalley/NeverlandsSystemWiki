The **Directors** are [[Players]] that hold the power of [[Arbitration]]. The [[Tables|Table]] agrees on Directors before starting any session of the [[Neverlands System]]. Once Directors have been decided, preferably with unanimous or near-unanimous consent, Directors assume the role of peacekeeper and make the final decision on every [[Statements|Statement]] played to the [[Racks]].
