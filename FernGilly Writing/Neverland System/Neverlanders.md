A [[Neverlanders|Neverlander]] is an egregore; they are thoughts given physical form and manifested into our world. Thought-forms like Neverlanders are flexible things; they assume a new and different [[Blessings|Blessing]] from [[Narrative Awareness System|N'as]] during [[Intermissions]], taking on different roles inside the [[Narratives|Narrative]] as the plot demands. Neverlanders are generally ageless creatures; they cannot be killed as long as the idea they are based upon continues to exist.

Neverlanders derive more than their attitude and personality from their base concept; they also derive power from that concept's popularity. The fear of death, for example, might produce a powerful Neverlander with the touch of death or the visage of a Grim Reaper. Neverlanders thankfully reside in the [[Astral Sea]], living on [[Constellations]] narratively far from [[Earth]]... but there are exceptions to the rule.

Sometimes, these Neverlanders formed from negative emotions spill out into the [[Mortal Realm]], forcing [[Mages]] to fight them back and force them back into the Neverlands. At times, these Mages form pacts with Neverlanders; these pacts result in the creation of [[Divines]], powerful half-Neverlanders with a [[Mortals|Mortal]] body. Divines are not necessarily a single individual; they may be made of a Neverlander providing direct assistance to their Mage counterpart, and may only be connected by a narrative [[Threads|Thread]].

Neverlanders do not consider their lives to be worth much, if anything at all; they are very willing to die or perish for a time to sell a gag. They do value the lives of Mortals greatly, however; devouring a Mortal can produce a Divine without a pact. Neverlanders who completely devour their Mortal halves or contract with a _dead_ Mortal are [[Echoes]], pale imitations of the real thing left to join the [[Cycle of Suffering]] or rot in the [[Lunar Constellation]]. 

Ideas of [[Actors]] are usually not just Neverlanders, but mirror-demons from the [[Catron Constellation]].  They are incredible fans of the person they are based on, to the point of stalking and collecting all the memorabilia of that individual; when an Actor perishes, these Neverlanders get first dibs at becoming their Echo. If an Actor has freed their mirror-demon, as a [[Council of Knots|Councilor]] might, they no longer have a reflection.
