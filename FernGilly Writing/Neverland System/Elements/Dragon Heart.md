**Dragon Hearts** are the various bureaucratic institutions that exist within a [[Dragon-Temples|Dragon-Temple]]. In a forest, these would be the [[Mother Trees]], if not arboreal management services in a nearby [[Dragon-Temples|Dragon-Temple]]; in a city, this would be the city hall or the nunnery of the local [[Priestesses]].

Dragon Hearts keep copies of the [[Accords]] for new Priestesses to review and modify; they also serve as a seat from which Priestesses can manage the various [[Mortals]] that live in their [[Dragon-Temples|Dragon-Temple]]. Dragon-Temples who form [[Marionettes]] may also choose to live within their Dragon Hearts, since they tend to be the safest [[Sanctuaries]] in the [[Dragon-Temples|Dragon-Temple]].

Only an [[Dances|Apocalypse]] can subvert the defenses of a Dragon Heart and lay a hand on the Accords of the Dragon-Temple. With that said, losing control of a Dragon Heart is a very real risk Dragon-Temples face; if their Dragon Heart should fall into the hands of their enemies, those foes may freely [[to alter|alter]] their Accords into unfavorable conditions for the Dragon-Temple. Cities are known to plunder the Hearts of the surrounding wilderness, taking over and depriving other Dragon-Temples of their ability to self-govern.
