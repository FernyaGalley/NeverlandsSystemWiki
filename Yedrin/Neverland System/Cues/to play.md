*Tenses (v): played (past), play (present), playing (progressive)*
*Laconic: To put something into play.*

To **play** an [[Game Mechanics|Element]] of the [[Neverlands System]] is to place that Element in the [[Racks|Rack]] or [[Canons|Canon]]. Playing an Element creates a [[Statements|Statement]] which also [[to imply|implies]] that the Element *exists* and should be reviewed by the [[Tables|Table]]. The [[Directors]] have the final say as to whether or not an Element should be [[to canonize|canonized]] after being played.
## Feature - (Played)
*see also* [[to pocket|(Pocketed)]] (antonym)

When an [[Game Mechanics|Element]] is played for the first time, it is [[to imply|implied]] to have the (Played) [[Features|Feature]]. This Feature is unlikely to be [[Appropriateness and Relevance|relevant]] as all Elements in play have this Feature, but the Feature should be [[to denote|denoted]] if it is ever logical to do such.
