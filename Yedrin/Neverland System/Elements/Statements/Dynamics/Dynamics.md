Dynamics are [[Features]] that act as modifiers for [[Props]] and [[Positions]]. There are two kinds of Dynamics: [[Major Dynamics]] which broadly outline a series of [[Game Mechanics|Rules]] that a Prop must follow, and [[Minor Dynamics]] which help [[Players]] differentiate between different Props and determine that Prop's magical affinity. Both Major Dynamics and Minor Dynamics share a [[Pockets|Pocket]] with their Prop, and sometimes Major Dynamics can define where a Prop can be Pocketed. Players are encouraged to mix and match different Dynamics on the Props in their Pockets to form unique load-outs for their [[Actors]].
