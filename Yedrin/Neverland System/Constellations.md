*Laconic: An island in a sea of madness, a place to rest your boots.*

The **Constellations** (also known as the **Neverlands** or the **Myriad Constellations**) are the home of the [[Neverlanders]], magical places seated in the **[[Astral Sea]]** (aka the depths of outer space). The Constellations are [[abstraction|abstractions]], islands that only exist if you believe in them strongly enough. Legends say that [[Narrative Awareness System|N'as]] filled the Constellations with Neverlanders by accident; artificial creatures born purely from imagination, they formed when she tried to imagine a world herself. Thankfully, whatever force animates these creatures doesn't depend on her attention-- brave [[Mortals]] and brazen [[Elves]] continued [[to breed]] in her absence, introducing millions of inhabitants across the Myriad Constellations.

Constellations lack borders; when they need more land for their citizens, they simply [[to claim|claim]] additional [[Statements]] until there are new lands. Most citizens live within [[Dragon-Temples]], massive egregores that protect them from the harsh environment of the Astral Sea. If claiming Statements proves too slow to accomplish their goals, Constellations will entreat their Dragon-Temples to breed and make smaller settlements that citizens can live inside for now.

Constellations develop cultures and technologies independently of one another; they are not averse to working with foreigners, but technology that works well in one [[Cults|Cult]] may fail in a different cult. Since magic is based in belief, societies must believe that foreign spellcraft works in ways that make sense to their cultural identity.

Each society worships their Dragon-Temples in different ways; most often, Constellations form pantheons. Citizens of the Dragon-Temples are tasked to pay tribute to their home Dragon-Temple, with each Dragon-Temple demanding its own form of tribute. As such, the local [[Theory of Economics|economies]] of Constellations tend to reflect the special interests of local Dragon-Temples. A Dragon-Temple who demands famous works of art as tribute invariably finds herself in the company of talented artists.

Constellations are also home to their own unique blends of [[Flora]] and [[Fauna]]. The [[Dragon Breath|Breaths]] of each Dragon-Temple in the Constellation are often related to one another, descendants of the first Neverlanders who chose to breed with Mortals. As such, they tend to be a blend of fantasy and reality; examples include agaric caps with little hats, rabbits with unicorn horns, and clockwork golems in the shape of a door. Mortals should find these creatures both recognizable and alien, as each operate in ways unique to that Constellation's [[Lines]].

Every Constellation has established their own unique **Lines** called [[Accords]] detailing how they function; the rules of physics which make them different from other Constellations, the way they perceive magic and how magic operates. [[Tables]] are encouraged [[to improvise]] Accords and make adjustments as necessary to make their [[Narratives|Narrative]] work; maybe one Line isn't true today because the story the party wants to tell is different.

Each Cult understands magic differently, and each Constellation is shaped by that understanding of magic. The Flora and Fauna that spring forth from a Constellation's [[Inkwells|Inkwell]] have properties of the native Neverlanders as well as Mortal counterparts and obey the Accords of the Constellations. The people have an economy and religion built around the same Dragon-Temples they live inside; all of this comes together to shape a fantastic setting for any campaign!
