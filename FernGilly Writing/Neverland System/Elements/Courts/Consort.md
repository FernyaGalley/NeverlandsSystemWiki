*Laconic: Consorts are flexible and may change their roles during [[Intermissions]].*

The **Consort**, is the fifth [[Noble Courts|Noble Court]] of the [[Carrow Deck]]. Consorts share traits in common with the [[Peasant|Peasants]] of the [[Lower Courts]], but are capable of becoming a [[Witch]], [[Maiden]], [[Nomad]] or [[Knight]]. Consorts are highly valued by [[Neverlanders]], as their latent magical energies make them ideal contractors. Peasants are drawn to their magnetic charisma even before their latent magical energies are awoken; it is [[Opinions|rumored]] that the easiest way to identify a Consort is to look for those who have many admirers.

Consort [[Pockets]] may store [[Props]] from any [[Noble Courts|Noble Court]].
Consort [[Results]] are [[to wound|(Vulnerable)]] to [[to wick|wicking]].
Consort [[Actors]] rarely remain Consorts once [[to bond|bonded]] to a [[Neverlanders|Neverlander]].
Neverlander Consorts may permanently bond with another [[Consort]].
