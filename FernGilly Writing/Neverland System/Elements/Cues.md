*Laconic: A mechanically significant Statement made with magical thinking.*

A **Cue** is a [[Statements|Statement]] that can be [[to cast|cast]] by any [[Mages|Mage]]. Cues are the products of [[Rule of Magical Thinking|magical thinking]]; they represent real actions taken in the [[Mortal Realm]] by [[Mortals]] and have real consequences for the beings around them. A car might be thrown with telekinesis in a Cue, but driven straight into an Actor's body in the real world. Do not consider Cues to be 'fake' just because they are the products of magical thinking.

Cues are [[Game Mechanics|mechanical]] in nature; they are hard mechanical processes with consequences that follow from their [[to resolve|resolution]]. Cues are mechanically firmer than Statements and easier for the [[Tables|Table]] to track in [[Conflicts|Conflict]], so [[Players]] are encouraged to use Cues in Conflict when possible.

Players are also encouraged to look for alternate interpretations of the same Cue; [[to wick|wicking]] and [[to melt|melting]] Cues is a great way to poke flaws in opposing arguments and discover new methods to achieve your goals.
