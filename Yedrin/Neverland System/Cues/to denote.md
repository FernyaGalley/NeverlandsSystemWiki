*Tenses (v): denoted (past), denote (present), denoting (progressive)*
*Laconic: To write something down for clarity.*

To **denote**, a [[Players|Player]] at the [[Tables|Table]] takes note of something they've [[to observe|observed]] and [[to pocket|pockets]] that [[Notes|Note]]. The Note may be [[to introduce|introduced]] to an existing [[Statements|Statement]] to make it more clear for other Players. [[Directors]] are encouraged to denote all [[Appropriateness and Relevance|relevant]] Features attached to a Statement, as well as keep their own Notes in a journal or writing software the Table cannot easily [[to witness|witness]].

Note-taking has shown positive benefits for long-term memory and mental health, so we encourage all Players to get into the habit of taking notes!
## Feature - (Denoted)
*see also*

When a [[Features|Feature]] or [[Statements|Statement]] has been *properly* (Denoted), there should be a visible stamp, marker or [[Threads|Thread]] properly linking the Feature or Statement and the [[Notes|Note]]. Placing the Note in the same [[Pockets|Pocket]] as the subject of the Note is usually sufficient to consider a Feature or Statement to be (Denoted).
