Diresk is the second [[Sephirot|Sephirah]], one facet of the [[Narrative Awareness System|Goddess of Suffering, N'as]]. He is the spirit of discovery; he travels the world in search of new and interesting sights, [[to observe|observing]] and [[to relocate|relocating]] to new [[Positions]] at a steady pace.
