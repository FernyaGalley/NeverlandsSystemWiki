The Neverlands System is a gamified system of organization for narrative elements. Using the mechanics of the Neverland System, a group of one or more writers can sit at the same table and write a story where fairies fight fascists and mages form socialist communes. The magic of the Neverlands System is based on emotions, while the themes of the Neverlands System are based in mythology and popular fiction. It's a kitchen sink setting with a defined core that [[Players]] can play through if they wish, with rules designed to make running the setting simple for the [[Directors]] and combat fun and frantic. All [[Players]] can always act at the same time, engage with the same systems and leave their own unique impact on the [[Narratives|Narrative]] that forms.
