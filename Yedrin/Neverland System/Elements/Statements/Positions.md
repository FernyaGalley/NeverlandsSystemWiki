A **Position** is a *[[Theory of Clarity|clarifying term]]* for a [[Statements|Statement]]; they are interchangeable terms. Any Statement can be a Position, and all Positions are Statements.

Positions are Statements that can be **[[to hold|held]]** by an [[Actors|Actor]]; when [[Conflicts|Conflict]] begins, every Actor who [[to enjoin|enjoins]] themselves to Conflict must hold at least one [[Positions|Position]]. Positions are both metaphorical and physical; taking a Position [[to imply|implies]] a physical presence on the [[Stages|Stage]] and a metaphysical presence in [[Canons|Canon]].

Every Position is implied to have at *least* two [[Major Dynamics]]; these Major Dynamics should describe the [[Game Mechanics|Rules]] of the Position, in both a physical and metaphorical sense. It is the task of the [[Directors]] [[to extrapolate]] the behavior of a Position from its Major Dynamics. Most Position Rules are simple to extrapolate, but examples below can help [[to clarify|clarify]] the concept for readers.

* [[Foundations]] are likely to serve as firm footholds physically, and unshakable worldviews in a metaphorical sense.
* [[Crossovers]] are often physical apertures like doorways and windows, but they can also be metaphorical transitions between arguments.
* [[Starters]] can be stairwells with dozens of landings, but as a metaphor they're great for [[to challenge|challenging]] other Positions.
* [[Dead Ends]] lead to dead ends, and as a metaphor they are no different.
* [[Threads]] can act as physical bridges between two places, but they can also serve as a bridge between two disconnected arguments.
* [[Inkwells]] could be the source of water for a river, or they could be the  source of evidence for a litany of metaphorical arguments.
* [[Hills]] could be mounds of earth like their namesake, or they could be arguments that need to be defended and underpin an Actor's philosophy.
* [[Slopes]] can be ramps to ease someone down the stairs, or they could be a series of slippery arguments of increasing intensity.
* [[Hazards]] could be pits of spikes, or they could be the venomous barbs of a cruel and hateful [[Fascists|Fascist]]. 
* [[Quagmires]] could be quicksand, or they could be arguments that are self-defeating in nature.
* [[Alternatives]] could be firm handholds, or they could be a quick trip into the abyss. It's all about interpretation, from a metaphorical perspective.
