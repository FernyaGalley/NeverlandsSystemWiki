A **Dim Result** is a [[Results]] which indicates both partial failure and partial success. Dim Results occur when an [[Actors|Actor]] is [[Risk and Duress]] or acting without experience, but somehow manage to fumble together a working solution for their problem. Actors can perform better with guidance, and even a [[Dull|Dull Result]] can become a Dim Result if they use [[Candles]] or act in concert with their team. A [[to snuff]] could also turn a [[Bright|Bright Result]] into a Dim Result, as even masters of a craft still make mistakes now and then.

A shortage of vital materials or lacking tools could also influence and worsen the Result of [[Arbitration]]; there is such a thing as wrong place and wrong time. Dim Results should not be overwhelmingly negative, but indicate that progress will be slow or that opposing parties will be given more time to respond. In [[Conflicts|Conflict]], a Dim Result could indicate each side of the Conflict is evenly matched; the Actor who earned a Dim Result will need to use more risky Statements to make further progress in that Conflict.
