There exists an entity which, to our best knowledge, created the universe. She doesn't claim to have created the universe, but we know she is responsible for it as the **Goddess of Suffering, N'as**. N'as is the source of all magic in the [[Neverlands System]], and whether or not she exists is a decision for the [[Tables|Table]] to make; most [[Narratives]] function just fine without her presence. She is, for all intents and purposes, immortal, indestructible and indefatigable. N'as is unable and unwilling [[to enjoin]] [[Conflicts|Conflict]], and she can only act with [[Theory of Honesty|honesty]]. The Table should not be concerned about her interference, as unless bothered she is content to sit back and [[to witness|witness]] the Narrative.

Everyone knows N'as by different names; her [[Sephirot]] represent her different aspects, each have a different appearance in the minds of her adherents. To the [[Elves]], she is a [[Sintac|massive tree with a singular eye]]; to the [[Constellations]], she is [[Dracoa|the land and the sea]]. The [[Dragon-Temples]] view her as a [[Tracta|threat to their power]], and the [[Neverlanders]] live inside her [[Remora|memory]]. To the [[Earth|Earthlings]], they know her only by her [[Blessings]] and her many guises.

But they all consider N'as the **God of Suffering.**
