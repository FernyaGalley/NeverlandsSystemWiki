*Tenses (v): introduced (past), introduce (present), introducing (infinitive)*

To **introduce** a [[Features|Feature]] is to combine an [[Game Mechanics|Element]] with a [[Dynamics|Dynamic]]. Most often, introduction is used to place a new [[Minor Dynamics]] in a [[Pockets|Pocket]], even one currently occupied by a [[Props|Prop]]. Introduction must be approved by a [[Directors|Director]], since some Dynamics are incompatible with some Props; we leave determining the inappropriate combinations as an exercise for the reader, since Dynamics are both very broad and open to interpretation. There could be any possible number of justifications for allowing a combination of Prop and Dynamic under one circumstance but not another.

The antonym effect of introduce is [[to estrange]].
## Feature - (Introduced)
An (Introduced) Prop is one that has a Minor Dynamic or an additional [[Major Dynamics|Major Dynamic]]. Props do not always have Minor Dynamics, and props which lack Minor Dynamics are considered to be (Estranged) Props, even if they have a Major Dynamic. 

There are few circumstances under which a Prop could have multiple Dynamics, as most are powerful and should not be combined; we leave this problem as another exercise for the reader.
