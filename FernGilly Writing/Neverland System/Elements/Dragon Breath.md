The **Breath** of a [[Dragon-Temples|Dragon-Temple]] is the manner in which that Dragon-Temple supports [[Mortals]] who live within it. If the Mortals require oxygen to live, a Dragon-Temple's Breath will include oxygen. If they require food and sustenance, Dragon-Temples will endeavor to supply themselves with berries and dew; Mortals who are [[(Native)]] to a Dragon-Temple should be able to live off the Breath a Dragon-Temple provides.

Breath could be seen as the [[Assets]] of a Dragon-Temple's [[Cults|Cult]], with one key distinction; Breath is not simply provided for free. It is *earned* by [[Cultists]], requiring them [[to cast]] [[Cues]] to keep themselves nourished; a Mortal must breathe to draw air, but they must also hunt for food or pay their rent. Breath is hoarded and protected by Dragon-Temples, and even the most devout [[Priestesses]] must put in [[Appropriateness and Relevance|reasonable]] effort to attain Breath between [[Intermissions]]. Failure to procure any Breath may [[to inflict|inflict]] new [[Features]]!
